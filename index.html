About

    Cao Yuwen, lecturer and master's supervisor at the School of Information Technology, graduated with a doctoral degree from Keio University in Japan. In February 2018, it passed the evaluation by the Expert Committee of the National Scholarship Council and obtained the qualification for studying abroad on a national public assignment. In October 2018, it was evaluated by the Japanese government's MEXT Scholarship Expert Committee and was awarded MEXT Scholarship funding. He has made a series of innovative achievements in the fields of 5G millimeter wave communication, intelligent edge computing, wireless communication and network convexity optimization algorithm research, and published high-level research results in international first-class journals such as IEEE TCOM, IEEE TVT, IEEE WCL, IEEE Access, and IEICE TCOM. Since 2018, a total of 8 high-level journal papers have been published in SCI, as well as 19 international conference papers (4 CCF conference papers).
